Specialist Finance Recruitment


frcs is a specialist recruitment company in the Finance industry. We like to think we are good at it and have a reputation for good business practice, impartial advice, discretion
and confidentiality.

Clients:


frcs is a specialist consultancy providing recruitment and advisory services to the Finance industry, Asset Finance & Leasing in particular. For over 25 years we have sourced and introduced candidates for management, sales, technical and operational roles utilising our extensive network of contacts and a comprehensive understanding of the industry - finance products, asset-types and trends.

We can also act in confidence if you require representation, industry research or advice on acquisitions, disposals, mergers, joint ventures or investment opportunities.

If you are recruiting or operating within the Finance industry, our expertise will be invaluable.

﻿

Candidates:


If you have expertise in an industry, profession or specialism, financial or otherwise, we can assist with your search for suitable job opportunities.

frcs will research your industry and undertake marketing to a target list of companies and organisations to their most appropriate management contact.

We can also advise and suggest other options and ideas to assist your search.

This approach is ideal if you are currently employed and seeking a discreet or sensitive career move - our enquiries are totally confidential and we do not disclose your personal
or company details until authorised by you.

There is no charge for this service.
